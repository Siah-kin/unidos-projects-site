Our Mission

Transforming Soil, Transforming Lives

REGESOIL (Regenerate the Soil) is Unidos's flagship soil regeneration initiative that has won 4 major international awards. We combine vermicomposting, biochar production, and food forest establishment to transform degraded refugee settlement land into productive regenerative farms.

For decades, refugees have relied on chemical fertilizers that harm soil health and human wellbeing. REGESOIL shifts this paradigm by teaching farmers to regenerate soil through organic amendments, diverse planting, and ecological practices that improve water conservation, remineralization, soil structure, and beneficial microlife.

We're moving refugees from dependency on food aid to self-sufficiency through healthy, regenerated soil.

Core Components

REGESOIL Programs

Five integrated programs working together to regenerate soil and build food security

COMPONENT #1

Permaculture & Vermicomposting

2-week training program in permaculture design, food forest establishment, and worm composting. Farmers learn to process organic waste into nutrient-rich compost using African nightcrawler worms.

501 Farmers Trained
350/151 Women/Men
Learn More
COMPONENT #2

Biochar Production

Training farmers to produce biochar using Kon-Tiki flame curtain kilns. Biochar improves soil structure, water retention, and nutrient availability while sequestering carbon for centuries.

2,300+ Farmers Trained
100s Tons Produced
Learn More
COMPONENT #3

Food Forest Design

Teaching regenerative agriculture through multi-layered food forest design. Combining fruit trees, vegetables, herbs, and beneficial plants to create self-sustaining ecosystems that produce abundant food.

6 Food Forests
2,800+ Farmers Trained
Learn More
COMPONENT #4

Arborloo Compost Toilets

Ecological sanitation system that transforms human waste into nutrient-rich compost. After 6 months, the toilet is moved and a fruit tree is planted in the composted material, creating permanent soil improvement.

500+ Toilets Built
100% Waste Recycled
Learn More
COMPONENT #5

Farmer Training & Education

Comprehensive training combining theory and hands-on practice. Farmers learn regenerative agriculture principles, organic soil amendments, water conservation, pest management, and climate-resilient farming techniques.

2 weeks Training Duration
6 months Follow-up Support

Leadership

Founded by Paulinho Muzaliwa

Paulinho Muzaliwa

Founder & Executive Director, Unidos

Congolese refugee and permaculture pioneer who founded Unidos and REGESOIL in 2018 after WFP food rations were cut. What started with 15 people learning to grow food has grown into a movement training 3,000+ farmers in regenerative agriculture.

Winner of the Gene Dewey Award 2024 for outstanding service to humanity. Paulinho's vision: transform refugees from aid recipients to change makers through soil regeneration and sustainable agriculture.
