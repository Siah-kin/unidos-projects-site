**Date**: February 2026
**Purpose**: Document vetting system to incubate legitimate refugee-led startups across Africa while preventing fraud common with NGO funding

**Target**: Help projects achieve $500/month self-sustainability (covers 1-2 staff + materials + transport)

## üéØ The Fraud Problem

### Why African NGOs Face Trust Deficit

**Common Scams**:
1. **Ghost projects** ‚Äî Photos/testimonials stolen from other organizations
2. **Inflated metrics** ‚Äî "Trained 500 farmers" but only 20 exist
3. **Fund diversion** ‚Äî Grant money for equipment ‚Üí spent on personal use
4. **Duplicate funding** ‚Äî Same project pitched to 5 funders, all think they're sole supporter
5. **Zombie NGOs** ‚Äî Registration exists, no actual operations

**Result**: Donors skeptical of ALL African NGOs (legitimate projects suffer)

**Example from Unidos Experience**:
- Struggled to get fundraising support ("how do we know you're real?")
- Western NGOs favored despite weaker outcomes (brand recognition > actual impact)
- Had to prove legitimacy repeatedly (exhausting, slows growth)

## üîç Vetting Funnel Mechanics

### Stage 1: Application (Week 1)

**Basic Eligibility Criteria** (Auto-reject if fails):
- [ ] Refugee-led or refugee-serving (51%+ leadership from displaced communities)
- [ ] Registered NGO/CBO (legal entity, tax-exempt status)
- [ ] Operational 6+ months (not brand new, some track record)
- [ ] Regenerative focus (agriculture, renewable energy, circular economy, education)
- [ ] Committed to transparency (willing to share financial data, beneficiary contacts)

**Application Form** (10 questions, 20 minutes):
1. Organization name, location, registration details
2. Founder story (why started, personal connection to problem)
3. Current beneficiaries (number served, demographics, verification contacts)
4. Revenue sources (grants, sales, donations ‚Äî breakdown by %)
5. Financial transparency (willing to connect bank account, show treasury?)
6. Product/service description (what do you produce/provide?)
7. Sustainability plan (how reach $500/month within 12 months?)
8. References (2-3 verifiable contacts: funders, partners, beneficiaries)
9. Social media presence (Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter ‚Äî activity check)
10. Why join Unidos umbrella? (what support needed, what can contribute)

**Auto-scoring** (AI flags):
- Generic language (ChatGPT fingerprints ‚Üí red flag)
- Copy-paste from other orgs (plagiarism check ‚Üí reject)
- Unrealistic metrics ("trained 10,000 farmers in 6 months" ‚Üí fraud signal)
- No social media activity (ghost org ‚Üí investigate)

---

### Stage 2: Reference Verification (Week 2)

**Automated Checks**:
1. **NGO registration lookup** (verify legal entity exists)
2. **Social media activity** (3+ months active posting, real engagement)
3. **Google Maps presence** (physical address, photos, reviews if applicable)
4. **Domain verification** (website SSL, creation date >6 months ago)

**Manual Outreach** (Unidos staff contacts):
1. **References** (call/email 2-3 contacts listed in application)
   - Confirm relationship (did they actually fund/partner with this NGO?)
   - Ask for honest assessment (strengths, weaknesses, red flags?)
2. **Beneficiaries** (random sample, 3-5 people)
   - Confirm received services ("Did you attend training? When? What learned?")
   - Ask about quality ("Was it helpful? Would recommend to others?")
3. **Local government** (OPM, district officials if refugee settlement)
   - Confirm presence ("Is this NGO active in your area? Any issues?")

**Red Flags** (Reject if 2+):
- References don't respond or deny relationship
- Beneficiaries never heard of organization
- Local officials report complaints/fraud history
- Financial records don't match claimed revenue
- Social media engagement is fake (bots, purchased followers)

---

### Stage 3: Pilot Test (Month 1-3)

**"Prove It" Challenge** (Low-stakes trial):

**Task**: Use wizard to run ONE crowdfunding campaign
- Goal: Raise $500-$2,000 (achievable for legitimate org)
- Duration: 30 days
- Milestones: 3-4 deliverables (must provide photo/video evidence)
- Escrow: Funds held until milestones verified

**What We're Testing**:
1. **Can they deliver?** (complete milestones on time, quality acceptable?)
2. **Are beneficiaries real?** (HEROES detection via Telegram community, behavioral patterns)
3. **Is data truthful?** (cross-reference claims with on-ground verification)
4. **Do they engage authentically?** (respond to questions, transparent about challenges?)

**Pilot Outcomes**:
- ‚úÖ **Pass** (80%+ milestones completed, beneficiaries verified, engagement authentic) ‚Üí Advance to Stage 4
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Conditional** (60-79% completion, minor issues) ‚Üí Coaching + retry once
- ‚ùå **Fail** (<60% completion, fraud signals detected) ‚Üí Reject + ban from reapplying 12 months

---

### Stage 4: Full Onboarding (Month 4-6)

**Wizard Access Granted**:
- Tier: **Starter** ($25/mo, waived for first 6 months if pilot passed)
- Features: HEROES detection, PQI scoring, 3 campaigns/year
- Support: Weekly check-ins (Unidos mentor assigned)
- Training: M&E workshop (impact measurement, data collection)

**Vetting Score Assignment** (100-point system):

| Dimension | Weight | Scoring Criteria |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| **Financial Transparency** | 20 pts | Bank account connected (10 pts) + Monthly reports (5 pts) + On-chain treasury opt-in (5 pts) |
| **Output Delivered** | 30 pts | Pilot milestones completed (30 pts), partial credit for % completion |
| **Beneficiary Impact** | 25 pts | Beneficiaries verified (10 pts) + Behavioral engagement (10 pts) + Testimonial quality (5 pts) |
| **Sustainability** | 15 pts | Revenue >$200/mo (5 pts) + Pathway to $500/mo (5 pts) + Commercial viability (5 pts) |
| **Quality** | 10 pts | PQI score avg >75 (10 pts), proportional for lower scores |

**Interpretation**:
- **85-100**: Premium tier (eligible for larger grants, featured in donor network)
- **70-84**: Standard tier (full wizard access, standard support)
- **60-69**: Probationary (6-month review, coaching required)
- **<60**: Rejected (refund Starter fee if paid, exit umbrella network)

---

### Stage 5: Ongoing Monitoring (Month 7+)

**Quarterly Reviews**:
- Vetting score recalculated (based on 3-month data)
- Beneficiary spot checks (random sample verification)
- Financial audit (compare reported vs actual revenue)
- Community feedback (other umbrella NGOs flag concerns?)

**Tier Progression**:
- **Up**: 70 ‚Üí 85 (unlock Premium benefits, higher grant access)
- **Maintain**: Stay in tier (continue current support level)
- **Down**: 85 ‚Üí 70 (warning issued, coaching offered)
- **Exit**: <60 for 2 consecutive quarters (removed from network)

## üõ°Ô∏è Fraud Prevention Layers

### Layer 1: Cryptographic Verification (Can't Fake)

**On-Chain Data**:
- Treasury wallet (all funds flow visible on blockchain explorer)
- Merkle proofs (batch-level production data, cryptographically verifiable)
- Timestamp anchors (photos/videos hashed, immutable record of "when")

**Impossibility of Fraud**:
- Can't claim "trained 500 farmers" if only 50 wallet addresses exist
- Can't inflate revenue if on-chain treasury shows actual flows
- Can't backdate evidence if timestamp anchors prove creation date

---

### Layer 2: HEROES Behavioral Detection (AI Catches Patterns)

**Fraud Signals AI Detects**:
1. **Copy-paste responses** (generic "thank you" messages, no personalization)
2. **Burst activity** (silent for weeks, then 100 messages in 1 day ‚Üí gaming score)
3. **Ring behavior** (5 accounts always like/comment together ‚Üí collusion)
4. **ChatGPT fingerprints** ("I'm thrilled to share", "transformative impact" ‚Üí AI-generated)
5. **Ghost beneficiaries** (account created yesterday, no activity except voting ‚Üí fake)

**HEROES Legitimacy Signals**:
- Consistent engagement (daily/weekly activity, not bursts)
- Authentic language (typos, local dialect, personal stories)
- Spontaneous questions (asking for help, not just self-promotion)
- Cross-validation (other community members confirm relationship)

---

### Layer 3: EigenTrust Reputation Graph (Ring Detection)

**How It Works**:
- Every NGO in network rates others (trustworthy or suspicious)
- Algorithm detects collusion rings (Group A always rates each other 5/5, outsiders 1/5)
- Isolated nodes flagged (no one vouches for them ‚Üí potential fraud)

**Example**:
```
Unidos ‚Üí trusts RRF (80%) ‚Üí trusts SINA (75%)
SINA ‚Üí trusts Unidos (85%) ‚Üí trusts NewNGO (40%)
NewNGO ‚Üí trusts self-created accounts (100%) ‚Üê RED FLAG
```

**Action**: NewNGO flagged for investigation (likely fraud ring)

---

### Layer 4: Passport Gate (Identity Verification)

**Multi-Factor Human Verification**:
1. **ERC-8004 passport** (on-chain identity, Sybil resistance)
2. **Telegram verified account** (phone number linked)
3. **X/Twitter account** (3+ months old, >50 followers, organic activity)
4. **Video call** (Unidos mentor meets founder, confirms real human)

**Anti-Sybil**:
- One person can't create 100 fake NGOs (biometric tie to identity)
- Stolen identities caught (cross-reference with government IDs if suspicious)

---

### Layer 5: Graduated Sanctions (Ostrom Model)

**Violation Tiers**:

| Violation | First Offense | Second Offense | Third Offense |
|-----------|--------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Minor** (late milestone, incomplete data) | Warning email | Coaching required | Vetting score -5 pts |
| **Moderate** (exaggerated metrics, poor quality) | Vetting score -10 pts | Probation (6 months) | Suspended (12 months) |
| **Severe** (ghost beneficiaries, fund diversion) | Suspended (12 months) | Banned (permanent) | Reported to authorities |

**Appeal Process**:
- NGO can contest suspension (submit evidence to dispute claim)
- Peer review (3 random umbrella NGOs vote on appeal)
- Majority vote overturns ‚Üí reinstate, otherwise ban upheld

## üí∞ $500/Month Sustainability Threshold

### Why $500/Month?

**Covers Basic Operations** (Refugee settlement context):
- 1-2 staff salaries: $200-$300/month (local wages)
- Materials/supplies: $100-$150/month (seeds, tools, packaging)
- Transport: $50-$100/month (tricycle, motorcycle fuel)
- Communications: $20/month (airtime, internet)
- Buffer: $30-$50/month (emergencies, unexpected costs)

**Market Validation**:
- If clients/beneficiaries won't pay $500/month value ‚Üí not solving real problem
- If can't reach $500/month in 12 months ‚Üí model not viable

**Self-Sustainability**:
- No perpetual grant dependency (can survive without external funding)
- Reinvestment possible ($100-$200/month back into operations)
- Growth trajectory (Year 2: $1K/month, Year 3: $2K/month, etc.)
