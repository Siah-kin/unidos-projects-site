### 5. **VETTING_FUNNEL.md**

**Category**: Strategy
**Status**: ‚úÖ Active (fraud prevention framework)

**Contents**:
- **The fraud problem**: Ghost projects, inflated metrics, fund diversion common with African NGOs
- **3-phase credibility building**: Prove Unidos ‚Üí Pilot vetting ‚Üí Scale vetting
- **5-stage vetting funnel**:
  1. Application (auto-reject fraud signals: ChatGPT fingerprints, plagiarism)
  2. Reference verification (call funders, beneficiaries, local government)
  3. Pilot test ($500-$2K crowdfunding, prove delivery)
  4. Full onboarding (vetting score 0-100, tier assignment)
  5. Ongoing monitoring (quarterly reviews, graduated sanctions)
- **Fraud prevention layers**: Cryptographic verification (can't fake on-chain data) + HEROES AI (catches copy-paste, ring behavior) + EigenTrust (collusion detection) + Passport gate (identity verification) + Graduated sanctions (warnings ‚Üí suspension ‚Üí ban)
- **$500/month sustainability threshold**: Covers 1-2 staff + materials + transport, proves market validation
- **Pan-African expansion**: Regional hubs (East/West/Southern/North Africa), localized vetting standards
- **Success metrics**: 10% pass rate (100 applications ‚Üí 10 graduates), <5% fraud rate, 95% donor trust by Year 5

**Key Insight**: "Unidos cannot vet others until it proves itself first" ‚Äî Earn credibility via cryptographic self-verification before scaling vetting network

**Use Case**: Umbrella NGO partner selection, fraud prevention protocols, donor trust building

## üîç Strategic Frameworks

### 1. **Compounding Loops Model**

Unidos is the only refugee NGO with **4 interconnected compounding loops**:

```
ECOLOGICAL LOOP
Vermicompost/Biochar ‚Üí Soil improves ‚Üí Yields increase ‚Üí More waste ‚Üí More vermicompost
          ‚Üì
FINANCIAL LOOP
B2B sales ‚Üí Revenue ‚Üí Equipment investment ‚Üí 10x production ‚Üí Market dominance
          ‚Üì
SOCIAL LOOP
Euler pool ‚Üí Cooperation scores ‚Üí DAO governance ‚Üí Ownership ‚Üí Better decisions
          ‚Üì
DATA LOOP
PQI scoring ‚Üí 5 years data ‚Üí Competitive moat ‚Üí Premium pricing ‚Üí Market access
          ‚Üì
ALL LOOPS FEED BACK INTO ECOLOGICAL LOOP (closed system)
```

**Multiplier Effect**: 16x growth per cycle (4 loops √ó 2x each) vs competitors with 2-4x (single/double loops)

### 2. **Fair Chain Optimization via PQI**

**Problem**: WFTO fair trade certification costs $2,000/year + audit fees (prohibitive for refugee NGO)

**Solution**: PQI scoring system
- **Cost**: $0 (built into SIAH)
- **Speed**: Real-time (vs annual audits)
- **Granularity**: 100-point scale (vs binary certified/not)
- **Verification**: Blockchain Merkle proofs (cryptographically verifiable)
- **Result**: 30-50% premium pricing (same as fair trade, but faster/cheaper)

**Formula**:
```
High PQI Score (85+) ‚Üí B2B Premium Pricing ‚Üí Higher Farmer Revenue
    ‚Üì
Higher Revenue ‚Üí Reinvestment in Regenerative Practices
    ‚Üì
Better Practices ‚Üí Higher PQI Score ‚Üí Loop Closes
```

### 3. **Vetted Grant Funnel via SIAH Wizard**

**Traditional NGO**:
- Manual grant writing (weeks of staff time, $5K+ cost)
- Self-reported data (donors must trust)
- 10-20% approval rate
- 6-12 months to funding

**Unidos + SIAH**:
- Wizard auto-generates (20 minutes)
- Cryptographically verifiable (Merkle proofs, on-chain treasury)
- 40-60% approval rate (instant trust via blockchain verification)
- 1-3 months to funding

**Equalizer Effect**:
```
Western NGO (familiar, self-reported) vs Refugee NGO (unknown, blockchain-verified)
    ‚Üí Refugee NGO wins (higher trust via cryptographic proof)
```

## üéì Key Lessons Applied

### From Bonzi_v5
- ‚úÖ **Water Prompt**: No buzzwords, mechanics first, specific metrics
- ‚úÖ **Wizard system**: 3-layer triage (Technical/Emotional/Business ‚Üí ProjectStatus/Sentiment/Impact)
- ‚úÖ **HEROES detection**: Behavioral trust scoring (Helper, Educator, Reflector, Organizer, Engager, Supporter)
- ‚úÖ **Euler pool**: O(1) gas distribution, proportional to contribution not purchase
- ‚úÖ **DAO governance**: Trust-weighted voting, sealed measurement, anti-gaming

### From Refugee NGO Research
- ‚úÖ **Regenerative compounds**: AWR 80% yield doubling, soil health improves over time
- ‚úÖ **Social enterprise self-funds**: SINA fee-for-service, Village Enterprise 534% ROI
- ‚úÖ **Market access scales**: MADE51 3,700 artisans, global fair trade
- ‚úÖ **Refugees can lead**: RRF refugee-founded, SINA refugee-replicated
- ‚úÖ **Crypto can fund (but volatile)**: Hyperskids 30 children via Stake2Earn, but -31% in 24h

### Applied to Unidos
- ‚úÖ **B2B revenue foundation** (not crypto speculation)
- ‚úÖ **PQI scoring** (fair chain without expensive certification)
- ‚úÖ **Cooperation score** (earn governance power by helping, not buying tokens)
- ‚úÖ **Progressive onboarding** (Telegram first, wallet later)
- ‚úÖ **Quadruple compounding** (ecological + financial + social + data loops)
