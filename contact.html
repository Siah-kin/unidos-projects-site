FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Unidos?

You can donate through our website or contact us directly for bank transfer details. Every contribution directly supports our training programs and community development initiatives.

Can I visit Nakivale Refugee Settlement?

Yes, we welcome visitors who want to see our work firsthand. Please contact us in advance to arrange a visit and ensure proper coordination with settlement authorities.

How can my organization partner with Unidos?

We're always open to partnerships that align with our mission. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities in agriculture, education, or community development.

Do you accept volunteers?

We occasionally accept volunteers with specific skills that can benefit our programs. Please reach out with your background and how you'd like to contribute.
