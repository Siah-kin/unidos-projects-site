## REGESOIL: Award-Winning Flagship Program

**REGESOIL (Regenerate the Soil)** is Unidos's flagship soil regeneration program that has won 4 major international awards:

### The 4 REGESOIL Awards:
1. üèÜ **Ockenden International Prize 2024** - ¬£25,000 (selected from 174 entries worldwide)
2. üèÜ **UNHCR Refugee-led Innovation Fund 2022** - Selected from 1,500 applicants (0.07% selection rate)
3. üèÜ **Social Impact Award Uganda 2022** - Business model incubation in Austria
4. üèÜ **Re-Alliance Camp Composting Grant 2022** - $5,000 seed funding

### Program Components:
- **Vermicomposting**: 7.8 tons/year organic fertilizer, 800 kg food waste processed weekly
- **Biochar Production**: 6,100 kg produced in 2025 using locally-fabricated Kon-Tiki kilns
- **Food Forest Design**: 4 demonstration sites on 15 acres, permaculture training

### Verified Impact (UNHCR 2024 Report):
- 501 farmers trained (350 women, 151 men)
- 1,172 people impacted indirectly
- 270 farmers: up to 45% yield increases
- Soil water retention improved 40% (drainage: 10 cm/hour ‚Üí 6.5 cm/hour)
- Crop diversity: monoculture ‚Üí 12+ crop polyculture

### Related Programs (Not REGESOIL):
- **Mushroom Spawn Lab**: 152 women trained, 4,800 kg mushrooms produced (2024)
- **Women's Circle**: 38 participants, menstrual health education
- **Arborloo Compost Toilets**: 50 planned, ecological sanitation (complementary to soil regeneration)
