# Unidos Projects - Email Setup Guide

Welcome to your new @unidosprojects.com email!

## Your Login Details

- **Your email**: (the address you were given, e.g. daya@unidosprojects.com)
- **Your password**: (the temporary password you received)

---

## Option 1: Webmail (Browser)

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
2. Go to **privateemail.com**
3. Enter your full email address and temporary password
4. You will be asked to change your password on first login
5. Done! You can now send and receive emails

---

## Option 2: Phone Setup (Android)

1. Open **Settings** → **Accounts** → **Add Account** → **Email**
2. Enter your full email address and password
3. Choose **IMAP** (not POP3)
4. Enter these settings:

**Incoming mail:**
- Server: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Port: `993`
- Security: `SSL/TLS`

**Outgoing mail:**
- Server: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Port: `465`
- Security: `SSL/TLS`

5. Tap **Done**

---

## Option 3: Phone Setup (iPhone / iPad)

1. Open **Settings** → **Mail** → **Accounts** → **Add Account**
2. Tap **Other** → **Add Mail Account**
3. Enter:
   - Name: Your name
   - Email: your @unidosprojects.com address
   - Password: your password
4. Tap **Next** and choose **IMAP**
5. Enter these settings:

**Incoming mail server:**
- Host name: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Username: your full email address
- Password: your password

**Outgoing mail server:**
- Host name: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Username: your full email address
- Password: your password

6. Tap **Save**

---

## Option 4: Gmail App (Android or iPhone)

1. Open the **Gmail** app
2. Tap your profile picture (top right) → **Add another account**
3. Choose **Other**
4. Enter your full email address and tap **Next**
5. Choose **Personal (IMAP)**
6. Enter your password
7. Enter these settings:

**Incoming server:**
- Server: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Port: `993`
- Security type: `SSL/TLS`

**Outgoing server:**
- Server: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Port: `465`
- Security type: `SSL/TLS`

8. Tap **Done**

---

## Option 5: Outlook (Computer)

1. Open Outlook → **File** → **Add Account**
2. Enter your full email address → click **Advanced options**
3. Check **Let me set up my account manually** → click **Connect**
4. Choose **IMAP**
5. Enter:

**Incoming mail:**
- Server: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Port: `993`
- Encryption: `SSL/TLS`

**Outgoing mail:**
- Server: `mail.privateemail.com`
- Port: `465`
- Encryption: `SSL/TLS`

6. Enter your password and click **Connect**

---

## Important Tips

- **Change your password** after your first login
- Use a **strong password** (mix of letters, numbers, and symbols)
- **Storage**: You have 45 GB of email storage
- If you forget your password, contact the admin (info@unidosprojects.com)
