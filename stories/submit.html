Your Story

Community Member

Are you part of the Unidos community? Have our programs changed your work, your farm, or your family? We want to hear from you - in your own words, in any language.

Submit your story below

Tell Us Your Story

Choose the way that works best for you. All stories are reviewed by the Unidos team before publishing. You keep ownership of your words and photos.

Email

Send by Email

Write your story in an email to info@unidosprojects.org. Attach photos if you have them. Write in English, French, Swahili, Kinyarwanda, or Kirundi - we will translate.

 WhatsApp

Send by WhatsApp

Send a voice note, text message, or photos on WhatsApp. This is the easiest option if you are in Nakivale or have limited internet. We will write it up for you.

 Instagram

Tag Us on Instagram

Post your story on Instagram and tag @unidosprojects. We will feature the best community posts on the website and in our reports.
In Person

Tell Us at the Center

Visit Unidos Social Innovation Center in Nakivale. We can record your story together - on video, voice, or written - during any program session or open hours.

How It Works

1

You share

Send your story by email, WhatsApp, Instagram, or in person. Any language. Any length. Photos and voice notes welcome.

2

We review together

The Unidos team reads your story, translates if needed, and sends you a draft. Nothing gets published without your approval.

3

You approve

You see exactly what will be published. You can change anything - your name, your photo, your words. You decide what the world sees.

4

Your story goes live

Published on the Unidos website with your name (or anonymous if you prefer). Linked to your Instagram if you want. You can request updates or removal any time.

Want to update your published story? Email info@unidosprojects.org or message us on WhatsApp with your changes. We update within 48 hours. You can also request removal at any time - your story, your choice.
