Unidos

Award-winning refugee-led organization in Nakivale, Uganda. Recognized by UNHCR, Ockenden International, and Lush Spring Prize. Since 2018: trained 3,000+ farmers in food forest design, processed 800 kg food waste weekly into worm compost, taught 700 women to grow oyster mushrooms and make organic soap.
