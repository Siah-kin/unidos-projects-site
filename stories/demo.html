This form would be a Google Form embedded on the site. Submissions go directly to a Google Sheet that the Unidos team controls. No coding needed to view or manage submissions.
Step 2 - What the Unidos admin sees (Google Sheet)

Admin Review Panel

This is the Google Sheet where all submissions land. Any Unidos team member with the sheet link can review.
