Why Donate

Your Impact Matters

Every donation directly supports refugees in Nakivale Settlement, helping them build sustainable livelihoods and transform their communities.

Unidos is a refugee-led organization, which means your donation goes directly to the people who understand the community's needs best.

$25

Provides seeds and tools for one family's food garden

$50

Trains one farmer in vermicomposting techniques

$100

Supports a woman through the soap-making business training

$500

Establishes a new vermicomposting system for 10 families

Unidos

Award-winning refugee-led organization in Nakivale, Uganda. Recognized by UNHCR, Ockenden International, and Lush Spring Prize. Since 2018: trained 3,000+ farmers in food forest design, processed 800 kg food waste weekly into worm compost, taught 700 women to grow oyster mushrooms and make organic soap.
